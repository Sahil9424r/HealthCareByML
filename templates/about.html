About Medical Diagnosis System

What We Do

The Medical Diagnosis System is an AI-powered tool designed to provide preliminary medical diagnoses based on symptoms input by users. Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze symptom patterns and identify possible conditions.

How It Works

Our system uses a Support Vector Classification (SVC) model that has been trained on a dataset containing various diseases and their associated symptoms. When you enter your symptoms, the model analyzes the pattern and matches it to the most likely conditions in our database.

The system then provides recommendations for precautions, medications, diet, and exercises based on the identified condition.

Important Disclaimer

This tool is designed for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.
